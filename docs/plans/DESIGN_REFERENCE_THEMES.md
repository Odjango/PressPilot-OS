## Cross-Vertical Insights

- **Astra FSE** appears in SaaS, E-commerce, and Portfolio — excellent single multi-category reference
- **Neve FSE** appears in Restaurant and Local Business — proven cross-vertical foundation
- Themes with 50+ patterns (Prime FSE, Neve FSE) offer the most composition variety
- For e-commerce, WooCommerce template files live in separate `woocommerce/` folder
