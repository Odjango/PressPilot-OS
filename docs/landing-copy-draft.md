## Key Benefits (Bullets)

### 1. Lightning Fast
> **90 seconds from upload to download.** No waiting for designers. No back-and-forth revisions. Just upload your logo, enter your business details, and get a complete theme instantly.

### 2. Truly Yours
> **Your colors. Your brand. Automatic.** PressPilot extracts your brand colors from your logo and applies them throughout—hero, buttons, headers, footer. No manual color picking.

### 3. WordPress Native
> **Works with any WordPress host.** Download a standard `.zip` theme that works with WordPress 6.0+. No proprietary lock-in. Full Site Editor compatible. Edit anything.

### 4. Industry-Smart
> **Layouts that make sense for your business.** Restaurants get menu sections and hours. Agencies get portfolio grids. Cafes get cozy, inviting layouts. PressPilot knows what you need.

### 5. Zero Dependencies
> **No premium plugins required.** Your theme runs on pure WordPress. No Elementor, no ACF, no page builder subscriptions. Just clean, fast, maintainable code.

## FAQ Snippets (for landing page)

**Q: Do I need to know how to code?**
> No. PressPilot generates ready-to-use themes. Just upload to WordPress and activate.

**Q: Can I customize the theme after?**
> Yes! Themes are fully editable in WordPress Site Editor. Change text, images, colors—anything.

**Q: What if I don't have a logo?**
> PressPilot works best with a logo, but you can generate a theme with just your business name and customize later.

**Q: Is this free?**
> Basic themes are free. Premium features (more layouts, priority support) require a subscription.
