Understanding Dental Implants: A Complete Guide

Dental implants are widely considered the gold standard for replacing missing teeth. Unlike dentures or bridges, implants are surgically placed into the jawbone, serving as artificial roots that provide a solid foundation for permanent or removable replacement teeth. This integration with the bone helps maintain facial structure and prevents bone loss that typically occurs when teeth are missing.

The Procedure Step-by-Step

  1. Consultation: Comprehensive 3D imaging and planning.
  2. Placement: Precision surgical placement of the titanium post.
  3. Osseointegration: A healing period of 3-6 months.
  4. Abutment: Attaching the connector piece.
  5. Restoration: Placing the final custom-made crown.
