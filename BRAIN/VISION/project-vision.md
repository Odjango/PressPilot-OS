# PressPilot – Project Vision

PressPilot is a web app that turns a small business’s information into production‑ready WordPress block themes using modern Full Site Editing (FSE) best practices.[file:32]

The app collects structured business data — name, logo, tagline, rich description, category, and special requirements — so AI generates content based on the real business, not only its name.[file:32] It supports text and image generation using third‑party services (e.g., OpenAI, Unsplash, DALL·E) depending on user tier.[file:32]

PressPilot’s core output is two WordPress themes containing:
- Block‑based templates and template parts that follow Gutenberg/FSE conventions for spacing, layout, and typography.
- Pre‑populated content and media that reflect the user’s business.
- Clean, extensible code ready for further customization in WordPress.

The app focuses on two high‑value verticals:
- E‑commerce sites that auto‑configure WooCommerce and generate a ready‑to‑run storefront when activated.
- Restaurant and café sites that generate structured menus, prices, and descriptions, including support for uploading menus from Word, text, or PDF and turning them into a navigable “Menu” section.[file:32]

Users see three homepage hero section options preview options with their own content before choosing one, then a generation animation and final screen with two downloadable site/theme options.[file:32]

All generated themes and the SaaS landing experience must:
- Use Google Fonts selections chosen by the user.
- Offer 5–7 curated color palettes derived from the uploaded logo, plus related alternatives.
- Support at least English, Spanish, French, Italian, and Arabic (RTL) in both the SaaS UI and generated sites.[file:32]

PressPilot’s long‑term goal is to become the fastest way for non‑technical business owners to obtain opinionated, high‑quality, multilingual WordPress sites that look custom‑designed but are generated in minutes.
