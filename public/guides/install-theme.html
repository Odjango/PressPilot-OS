Install Your Theme

Get your new WordPress theme up and running in minutes

1

Log into WordPress

Go to yourdomain.com/wp-admin and sign in with your admin account.

2

Navigate to Themes

In the left sidebar, click Appearance → Themes.

3

Upload Your Theme

Click Add New Theme at the top, then Upload Theme. Choose the ZIP file you downloaded and click Install Now.

4

Activate

Once installed, click Activate to make it your live theme.

5

Customize in Site Editor

Go to Appearance → Editor to customize colors, fonts, and content using the WordPress Site Editor.

Troubleshooting

  • Requires WordPress 6.0+ and PHP 7.4+
  • If upload fails, check your hosting's max upload size (should be at least 10MB)
  • Clear your cache after activating the theme
  • For WooCommerce sites, install WooCommerce separately after theme activation
